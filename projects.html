Shadow in the dungeon


This project is a concept for a turn-based RPG game, where the player explores dungeons composed of multiple floors and rooms. Each room offers access to two others, allowing the player to choose their path after each battle or event. The rooms are numbered, like 'Room 4, 5, 10', and each floor has a specific number of rooms as shown in the image.


The project is influenced by Buriedborne, Albion Online, and Dark and Darker. This high-risk MMO turn-based RPG severely penalizes player defeat, resulting in the loss of all their items.



WT MFD


WT MFD (Multi Functional Display) is a Chrome extension that simulates a real MFD, providing important and useful information for flights in War Thunder. The extension displays data such as:


Speed "mach" Altitude Fuel level Fuel flow Dynamic flight time VTOL angle Variable geometry wing angle Engine power Compass Real-time map

WT HMD


WT HMD HMD Warthunder is an overlay system for Warthunder that puts the most important flight information directly on your screen, just like a Helmet-Mounted Display (HMD). It also features Betty-style voice alerts, so you never miss a critical detail during the game. With a dynamic display, it only shows what really matters, making your flight experience more practical and immersive.


The app works using Electron as a base, utilizing JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. It collects information from Warthunder's localhost and displays it on the screen.
