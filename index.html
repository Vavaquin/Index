I am a pixel artist. Since the beginning of 2023, I have been exploring the fascinating world of pixel art, transforming my passion for games into visual creations.

Projects

Shadow in the dungeon


This project is a concept for a turn-based RPG game, where the player explores dungeons composed of multiple floors and rooms. Each room offers access to two others, allowing the player to choose their path after each battle or event. The rooms are numbered, like 'Room 4, 5, 10', and each floor has a specific number of rooms as shown in the image.


The project is influenced by Buriedborne, Albion Online, and Dark and Darker. This high-risk MMO turn-based RPG severely penalizes player defeat, resulting in the loss of all their items.

Comissions

34x


Pixel arts of 32x32, 36x36, and 37x37 pixels are quite common due to their simplicity and extreme usability. These dimensions are emblematic of the "Pixel Art" style because of their low pixel count, which does not diminish their elegance.



How much does it cost?


34x = $15




"It is worth noting that Twitch uses a standard of 28x28 pixels and its multiples x56 and x112"

64x


Pixel arts of 56x56, 64x64, and 66x66 pixels are more detailed, making them suitable for portraits and elements that require greater detail. They are widely used because they manage to combine richness in detail with the simplicity characteristic of pixel art.


How much does it cost?


64x = 20$

128x


Pixel arts of 128x128 pixels are especially good for representing full-body characters. If 64x64 pixels are a good parameter for creating portraits, 128x128 pixels are ideal for drawing a character's or for creating even more detailed and refined portraits.


How much does it cost?


128x = 45$
