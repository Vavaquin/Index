Pixel art

Pixel art is a style of digital art where images are created or edited at a very detailed level, manipulating each pixel individually. It originated in the early days of video games due to the graphical limitations of computers and consoles at the time. It is characterized by simple graphics, limited color palettes, and stylized shapes. Today, it is widely used in indie games, retro-style illustrations, and animations, being appreciated both for its nostalgic aesthetic and its artistic complexity.
